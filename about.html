About

My Story

In my mind empathy is key. I implement that mindset in all that I do; whether that be in my design work or showing up for my community in the San Francisco Bay. I have a B.S. in Environmental Science with a minor in Chemistry, as well as a certificate in UX design with a specialization in Frontend Development. I have a background in working collaboratively in a variety of indoor and outdoor settings. I am a fast learner and always up to the task of passing my knowledge onto others. My previous experience in the environmental world in combination with design has molded me into a person with strong problem solving abilities, leadership skills, and humility.
