TatThat

An application where users can try out a tattoo design before they make it permanent; as well as find a sense of community while being able to explore tattoos artists and collectors near them.

A competitive analysis was completed, along with user interviews, created personas, developed an evolving site map, followed by low-fidelity through high-fidelity prototypes. Multiple iterations of TatThat were tests in usability sessions.
